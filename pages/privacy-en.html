van Rie bv is responsible for processing personal data within the context of our business activities.

We only process the personal data that is strictly necessary, such as:

  • Any other data necessary for processing your orders and accounting.

3. Purposes of the processing

Your personal data is only processed for:

4. RLegal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the following legal bases:

5. Storage Period

Your data will be stored as long as necessary for the purposes mentioned above, and in accordance with legal retention periods (for example, 7 years for accounting records).

6. Sharing Data

We only share your data with third parties when necessary, such as:

  • Legal authorities if required (for example, the tax authorities).

In all other cases, your data will not be shared with third parties without your consent.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data;
  • Have incorrect data corrected;
  • Have your data deleted, unless this conflicts with legal obligations;
  • Object to certain processing operations.

For questions or requests regarding your personal data, you can contact us via info@vanrie.be.

8. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against loss, unauthorised access or misuse.

If you have any questions about this privacy statement or about the processing of your personal data, you can reach us at info@vanrie.be of van Rie bv, Stationsstraat 5, 9990 Maldegem.
